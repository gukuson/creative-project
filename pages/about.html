About Me

I was born and raised in Utah, so naturally I love the outdoors. What started out as a simple hobby quickly turned into my passion for the last 8 years. Since then, photography has always been my first love. Early in my college career my friends began asking me to photograph their portraits/families/weddings and from then on I was hooked. Soon enough I phased out working mind-numbing 9 to 5 jobs and became a full-time photographer and videographer. I currently shoot freelance portraits and weddings, and am the lead videographer for the University of Utah Department of Athletics. I’m living my childhood dream and couldn’t be happier.
