About Us

Welcome to Anthony's Helpers. We are focused on providing a simple, easy process to help you move. We know from personal experience how hard moving can be, especially when you don't have the right equipment or manpower. Luckily, we provide both of those for you.

My name is Stanton Anthony and I recently started this business. After helping a few of my family members move, I was annoyed by how expensive other moving comapnies are. So I decided to create an affordable, but also reliable moving service to help everyone else who felt the same.

I love playing volleyball, ping pong, racquetball, travelling the world, and spending time with my girlfriend Kaylee. I am sure you have similar hobbies and interests and moving probably isn't one of them. You shouldn't have to stop doing what you love just because you are moving. That's why we are here.
